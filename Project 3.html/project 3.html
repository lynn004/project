About

I'm a writer still in training based in Kenya and writing has always been my passion ever since I was little.I take lots of pride in writing since it takes one to a whole new world full of fantasies where we all get to write our own stories just the way one would like to put it. Besides writing I also enjoy other activities such as listening to music since I've always considered music to be my source of inspiration.I also love travelling a lot because it's always an opportunity to explore different regions in the world and learn about whole new stuff which is very much exciting!

Poetry

With poetry, it's a quite and calm way of expressing thoughts and emotions hence something I really love to do.

Here's a sample of a poem I wrote:

The salty droplets
The bright smile
The beautiful pictures
The cheeky words
The playful nature
All have something beneath
A hug to embrace
